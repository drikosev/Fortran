
 To upgrade 'fcheck' from OpenMP (4.5) to (5.0) a long list of changes are required. So,
 this separate ChangeLog document may keep things clear. The changes required affect, the
 Scanner, the Parser, and the Translator. Obviously, it's the minimum effort required just 
 for parsing.
